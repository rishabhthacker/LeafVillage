Our Journey

Could it be any more ambitious? It all started with one simple thought: ‘Let’s bring nature into people’s homes—even if their track record with plants is basically plant-icide.’ What began as a deep-rooted passion for plants and a mild obsession with keeping them alive has somehow spiraled into a full-blown mission to greenify the world—one slightly panicked, overwatering customer at a time.

The Seed of an Idea

In 2024, we had a huge realization—people were so busy scrolling through their phones, they forgot trees even existed. So, we took action. We created a space where plant lovers (and those who just want to seem like plant lovers) could find not just plants, but also a crash course on how not to kill them. You know, unlike Toby, who just sucks the life out of everything—including plants. I gave him a succulent once… it died out of boredom.

Growing Together

We started small—like, really small. Like, ‘Is this a leaf or did I drop my lunch?’ small. But after a few sleepless nights, a couple of plants that, uh… let’s just say moved on to a better place, and a whole lot of trial and error, we built a collection of plants and gardening tools that are basically Phil-proof. Which means you probably can’t screw them up either… hopefully. No guarantees if you’re a Dunphy, though.

Blossoming into a Community

As we grew, so did our community of plant enthusiasts—some thriving, some… well, let’s just say their plants entered witness protection. We’ve seen it all—from ‘I’ve never bought a plant in my life’ to ‘I talk to my plants more than actual humans.’ And yeah, we’ve helped them all. We’ve also learned a thing or two along the way—like how Boyle once tried to marinate his basil plant because he thought it would ‘infuse the flavor from the inside.’ Yeah… don’t be a Boyle. But together, we’ve built a space where creativity and sustainability meet… and hopefully, you won’t commit plant-icide.

Our Mission

"Let’s make the world greener, one plant at a time... because, clearly, the planet needs more of us trying to keep plants alive."

YO CHAT! Leaf Village goin’ CRAZY right now! We all about that greenery, you feel me? Our mission? Turn your space into a vibe, boost your mood (or at least distract you from the Ls of life), and save the planet one plant at a time. Lowkey, we’re like the fairy godmothers of plant life… but with more drip.

This started as a tiny, probably delusional dream, and now? We GLOBAL. We got the finest plants (that hopefully won’t die on you), advice that won’t have you feelin’ like you in a college lecture, and we deliver straight to your door like the plant plug you never knew you needed.

Whether you’re a plant expert or you just Googled ‘Why my succulent lookin’ sad,’ we gotchu. Eco-friendly, stress-free, and backed by a whole community of plant lovers who probably know more than you. But it’s all love—WE IN THIS TOGETHER!
